## Where to get logos:

- **Official brand kits**: Most companies provide official logos in their brand/press kits
- **Simple Icons**: https://simpleicons.org/ (free SVG icons for popular brands)
- **Worldvectorlogo**: https://worldvectorlogo.com/
- **Brands of the World**: https://www.brandsoftheworld.com/

## Logo specifications:

- Format: SVG (preferred) or PNG with transparent background
- Size: Logos will be displayed at 48x48px
- Color: Use the official brand colors (colorful versions)
- Background: Transparent

## Alternative:

If you don't have the logos yet, the component will show placeholder icons until you add the actual logo files.
